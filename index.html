About 2B Electrical LLC

We are a locally owned and operated electrician business serving Longmont, CO (80015) and the surrounding areas.

Owned and operated by Donald R. Bolton, we specialize in comprehensive electrical upgrades, diagnostics, and customized installations. Holding a Master Electrician License (ME.0600806) and a Colorado State Electrical Contractor License (EC.0007662), Donald guarantees adherence to local codes and safety protocols.
